Fast Space Facts

Science Basics

Key Ideas Made Simple

Rotation

Rotation means a planet spins on its own axis. One complete spin creates one day on that planet.

Revolution

Revolution means a planet travels around the Sun. One complete trip around the Sun creates one year.

Gravity

Gravity is the force that pulls objects toward each other. The Sun's gravity keeps planets in orbit.

Asteroid Belt

The asteroid belt is a region between Mars and Jupiter filled with rocky objects called asteroids.
